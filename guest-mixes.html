Nat Mac

We are BUZZING to feature the one and only Nat Mac on our Eve mix series. Hailing from Essex originally but now Cork based, not only is Nat a class selector but she also hosts her own radio show and is a director at Underground Ovation who are supporting and promoting club culture in our little country! GO NAT! We have seen her play many times and we are so excited she's done a mix for us!

Tamisya Smith

Tamisya is a Dublin based DJ who burst onto the scene earlier this year! Since then we have seen her name feature on line ups in Wigwam, The Bernard Shaw, Button Factory and many more. Tamisyas appreciation for old school dance peppered with her love for house, techno, disco and electronic music gives her sets a depth and this mix is no exception.

Aimie Mallon

Aimie Mallon needs no introduction. We first met her on Halloween Night at a rave in a Manor and she BLEW our minds. Being one half of electro-indie-house-pop duo LE BOOM Aimie is well known for her energetic live performances. She instantly commanded the entire room and filled it with some unbelievable techno!! What a rave it was! We are BUZZING to have Aimie feature on our Mix series!

Elaine

We are delighted to welcome the wonderful Elaine Graym to our Eve Mix series! Technomermaids

Introducing...... Technomermaids!! Amelie and Sandra, Two long time friends who joined forces behind the decks a year ago and proceeded to create an amazing vibe! Their party Shhh&Dance based down in Cork will be celebrating its 1st birthday in February next year :) This mix is a beautiful blend of melodic techno and some ravey goodness!! We are really excited to have them as our next instalment in the Eve mix series.

Lou Morley

A selector who has been playing and collecting records since her early teens, we are SO delighted to feature her on as a guest for our mix series. After taking a little break, we welcome Lou back to the decks with open arms! Her incredible music taste is undeniable and this recording tells you exactly why! Yasmin Gardezi

Making waves in Cork with her infectious energy on stage and her love of techno, Yasmin first popped up on our radar last summer after her cracking warm up set for Irelands very own Hybrasil. Having only started playing in the last year, Yasmin has already warmed up for Huxley and Klangkarussel! Sharing bills with Richy Ahmed, DJ Deece and Kettema so early into her DJ'ing career proves she in one to watch! We are delighted to have her as our 6th instalment of our guest mix series!
